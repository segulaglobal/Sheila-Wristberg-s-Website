Changing the narrative of leadership

The world today is at a unique crossroad, facing a blend of traditional challenges like poverty and inequality, whilst also grappling with new-age dilemmas like climate change and interesting opportunities like generative AI. The era of one-dimensional leadership is dead. Today’s leader must be multifaceted- that is adept in building a business, well-versed in technology, committed to social justice, whilst embracing sustainability and global-mindedness.
I am on a mission to build a pipeline of well-rounded leaders who not only focus on the present but also envision and shape the future.

As an award-winning CEO disrupting traditional norms and an Insurance trailblazer turned ESG advocate, I bring an unexpected lens to unlocking sustainable progress in Africa’s future. My career has traversed demanding roles from Big4 consultant to Pan-African insurance leader yet I chart my own course to stay grounded in my higher purpose – empowering emerging voices to drive change. I believe the world’s most complex challenges require leaders who can hold courageous vision while lifting diverse talents around them.


My passion for inclusive leadership led me to launch pioneering initiatives like Women Leadership in Insurance Africa, shaping an industry I’d built expertise in. As Vice Treasurer for the Insurance Brokers Association of Ghana, I’m now influencing national and continent-wide transformation from within key networks. I serve on multiple boards, and I consider visible young African role models crucial to shifting narratives so I share lessons from my story.


I speak on investing in women leaders for prominent global organizations but my core investment lies in mentoring youth one-on-one to unlock their purpose and capabilities. While my career charts ever-bigger visions, I find grounding in simple everyday moments that renew my sense of possibility – and my commitment to speak hope into people so they can create the change our world needs.

I may be known as an award-winning executive but I’m still that audacious girl who set her ambitions beyond society’s scripts. Today I stand on stages from Angaza Forum and conferences to Youth camps because I believe YOUR story holds power too. Let’s shift narratives by daring to dream differently – join me in making space for new voices to lead. Progress lies in who we lift up together.

Awards and Recognitions.

2023 AGYLE Fellow

I am 2023 African German Young Leaders in Business (AGYLE) Fellow, and I am proud to be part of a transformative initiative that bridges the gap between young African and German leaders in business, politics, and media. AGYLE is more than a fellowship; it is a dynamic platform fostering a long-term business network and driving the development of innovative business models. At its core, AGYLE seeks to strengthen the African-German dialogue, laying the foundation for robust cross-border economic cooperation. This fellowship represents a commitment to collaboration, innovation, and building connections that transcend borders, aligning perfectly with my vision for a more interconnected and prosperous future.

Chartered Insurance Institute of Ghana (CIIG) Young Insurance Professional of the Year, 2022

As the recipient of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Ghana (CIIG) Young Insurance Professional of the Year award in 2022, I am honored to be recognized for my contributions to the insurance industry. This accolade symbolizes a commitment to excellence and innovation in the field, reflecting my dedication to shaping the future of insurance in Ghana and beyond. It serves as a testament to the collaborative efforts of a passionate community working towards advancing the standards and impact of the insurance profession.

ANGAZA's Top 10 Women in Banking and Finance

I am proud to be showcased among Africa’s most groundbreaking women finance leaders by Angaza Forum after being ranked #2 in their Top 10 Women in Banking and Finance. This recognition from the continent’s premier platform for ethical financial services advancement celebrates my work in pioneering insurance. This esteemed recognition reflects not only my work at iRisk Management Ltd. or personal accomplishments but also underscores a shared commitment to promoting financial inclusion, ethical finance, and empowering women in leadership. ANGAZA Forum's dedication to showcasing achievements and building strategic leadership capacities aligns seamlessly with my vision for a more inclusive and empowered financial landscape. I leverage this visibility to inspire more leaders to bring their talent to reforming systems into ethical, people-first powerhouses.

WIMA Top 50 Awards Africa

Adding to my leadership honors, I was honored to join the Top 50 Women in Management Africa (WIMA) 2023, recognized for outstanding achievement amplifying African stories of excellence. This award celebrates my consistent and transformational leadership within my field but also amplifies the narrative of African excellence on a global stage.

YIPS- Africa Insurance Organization Next-Gen Insurance Awards.

Adding to my 2022 leadership honors, I was named 1st runner-up for the Young Insurance Professionals, Africa (YIPS-Africa) Insurance Organization’s Next-Gen Insurance Awards. This exclusive continental award shines a spotlight on the industry’s most pioneering talents under 35 who are redefining Africa’s future landscape. As the then Managing Director of iRisk Management Limited, I was recognized for repeatedly innovating and contributing to the growth and development of Insurance on the continent . While humbled by the global visibility, I graciously pass the torch to shine light on talented youth who still battle limits on realizing their full potential.
